Black Panther

They are able to jump about 20 feet vertically from a standing positon.

  • Gestation: The gestation period of Panthers is 3 to 4 months
  • Relaxing: Panthers like to dive to relax themselves
  • Cat Familia: Panthers are the fifth largest species of cats
  • Melanation: Dark color pigmentation mutation that makes the fur to be blackish

Black panther is the melanistic colour variant of any Panthera, particularly of the leopard in Asia and Africa, and the jaguar in the Americas.
